Data

Non-profit organizations have many different methods to choose from for how to communicate with their members. We asked a group of 22 individuals what method they preferred. More than half agreed that an email was best. None of those who took the survey choose postal mail as a method of communication.

Websites for nonprofit organizations have many components to get various information to the users. Though websites have different usages, when we asked 22 individuals what content they would like to see in a website, the top option that people choose was a calendar of events followed by having pictures from past events that the organization has had.

Communication with the website for members to contact each other is a necessity for non-profit organizations. There are multiple ways that they can implement member communication into the website. When we surveyed 22 individuals whether they preferred discussion boards, direct messaging, or secluded group chats, more than half of the participants choose direct messaging.
