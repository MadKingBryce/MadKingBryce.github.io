A Message From the CeO

John Dolan




"Like all of you, I am deeply disturbed by reports of our tires being discovered in protected wetlands in Louisiana. As expressed in Greenlight 2030, commitment to our planet through sustainable business practices is one of our company’s core commitments - one that we have all worked hard to live by for the almost 50 years that we’ve been in business."

-John Dolan

Crisis Spokesperson




"While the situation in the wetlands was unfortunate and ultimatelty unnecessary, we at Greenlight are proud of the massive strides we are making towards restoring the area to its former glory. Our cleanup crew is progressing at a massive pace and the improvement is very visable. We have disposed of all tires and are examining the wildlife for lasting damage, and to ensure that the ecosystem is unharmed. We are confident that our efforts will lead to a cleaner and healthy Louisiana Wetlands."

Liam Jaison
